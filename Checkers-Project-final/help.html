Only the dark squares of the checkerboard are used. A piece can only move diagonally into an unoccupied square. If a space behind the oponents piece is empty it can be taken. Game ends when no pieces are left, the timer runs out, or one player surrenders. When you move a piece to the other side it becomes a king that can move in both directions. If you jump a piece and on that same turn there is another space that same piece can jump you can double jump.
