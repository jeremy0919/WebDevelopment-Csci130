Dolphin social cognition

Training phase:


Understand that humans are creatures
Able to navigate surroundings
Taught standard hand signals using positive reinforcment
Learned at rapid speeds



Experimental phase


Testing social cognition of dolphins and how well they adapt to their environment
Testing Variables:time it takes to achieve outcome
Changing Variables: Location of instruction/ end goal
Constant Variables: reward

    • Interpretation of results Social Cognition

    Dolphins quickly adapted to their environemnt, not only able to react to different instructions but also realized that if both dolphins worked together they could achieve the goal the easiest. Started adaapting faster and faster together to changing instructions.

    MIRROR TEST

    Environment: Dolphins were put in a familiar enviornment with a mirror placed in front of them
    Observation: Dolphins weren't afraid or treat mirror like another animal instead made movements and viewed reflection, then looked at areas of their body they werent able to see
    Take away: Dolphins are highly intelligent and able to quickly grasp changes to their environment such as a mirror being a reflection instead of another dolphin
