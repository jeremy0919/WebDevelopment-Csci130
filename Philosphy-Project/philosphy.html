Training phase:

Understand that humans are creatures
Able to navigate surroundings
Taught standard hand signals using positive reinforcment
Learned at rapid speeds

Experimental phase

Testing social cognition of dolphins and how well they adapt to their environment
Testing var: time it takes to achieve outcome
Changing var: Location of instruction/ end goal
Constant var: reward
Interpretation of results
Dolphins quickly adapted to their environemnt, not only able to react to different instructions but also realized that if both dolphins worked together they could achieve the goal the easiest. Started adaapting faster and faster together to changing instructions.
